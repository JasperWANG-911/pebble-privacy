Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

Welcome to Pebble. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: University email (.ac.uk), username, user handle, and profile picture.
  • Location Data: Precise location data to enable map functionalities, nearby posts, and friend location sharing.
  • Content Data: The "Pebbles" (posts) you create, including text and images.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your university student status.
  • To show "Pebbles" posted near your location.
  • To facilitate social interactions like finding friends and sharing live location (if enabled).

3. Third Party Services

We use Google Firebase for secure authentication and data storage.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at: support@mypebbles.uk

Terms of Service

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

1. Acceptance

Access to Pebble is restricted to users with a valid university email address (e.g., ending in .ac.uk).

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

To maintain a safe community, we enforce a strict Zero Tolerance Policy. The following are strictly prohibited:

Prohibited Activities:
  • Illegal Activities: Drugs, weapons, violence, or unlawful acts.
  • Academic Dishonesty: Ghostwriting, exam cheating aids, or academic fraud.
  • Spam or Ads: Commercial advertising or promotions.
  • NSFW Content: Nudity, sexual content, or harassment.
  • Gambling: Betting or money laundering schemes.

Violations will result in immediate content removal and permanent account bans.
